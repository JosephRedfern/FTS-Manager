About

FTS is a student-led seminar series within the School of Computer Science at Cardiff University.

Talks are varied, and not strictly limited to topics within Computer Science -- some of the most interesting seminars have been entirely unrelated (such as a history of The Flamenco, tales of travelling the world, and insight into behind-the-scenes of an episode of Dr Who). The only rule of FTS is that speakers and attendees must be students (except for special circumstances).

If you're interesed in speaking, please complete the form here, or if you have any questions about FTS then please contact the current organisers Rhodri Morris and Wafi Bedewi.
